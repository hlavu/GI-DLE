(G)I-DLE
We love music

(G)I-DLE is a South Korean girl group formed by Cube Entertainment in 2018. The group consists of six members: Soyeon, Miyeon, Minnie, Soojin, Yuqi, and Shuhua. They debuted on May 2, 2018, with their debut single "Latata". The group has been praised for their unique concepts and musicality, and have gained a large following both domestically and internationally. They has been known for their unique and distinct concepts that set them apart from other K-pop groups.
